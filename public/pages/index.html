Current Research

Optimizing Pricing Strategy with Royalty Constraints

Mustafif Khan Status: In Progress • Expected 2025

This research develops a comprehensive nonlinear optimization framework for determining optimal pricing strategies in multi-platform publishing environments with complex royalty structures. The work addresses the challenge of simultaneous pricing across multiple distribution channels while respecting platform-specific royalty constraints.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Nonlinear optimization problem formulation for pricing strategy
  • Implementation using Sequential Least Squares Programming (SLSQP)
  • Trust-Region Constrained optimization methods
  • Transparent, data-driven pricing methodology for digital and print platforms
