Not-For-Profit Organization

The MoKa Reads Collective is operated under a not-for-profit structure and uses its funds to support the development of our open-source projects and research/publications.

What is MoKa Reads?

MoKa Reads is an organization that develops open-source projects and research/publications. We hope to be able to provide a platform for educational publications and research to be accessible under an opensource-like nature and develop open-source projects to apply some our research.

Our Mission?

MoKa Reads is founded by Mustafif Khan, who’s an independent author/publisher, Financial Mathematician, and open-source developer. His first published book was “Rust for C++ Programmers” and now wants to provide a platform which places publications and research papers under opensource licenses, and help build something amazing, and help others to learn it.

  • We hope to provide a platform for open publications and research.
  • We hope to provide a platform for open-source projects to be developed and maintained.
